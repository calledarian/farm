Common Questions About Donations

Your donation will help support our farm's sustainability efforts, including buying seeds, maintaining eco-friendly equipment, expanding farming practices, and covering the costs of artificial insemination and vaccinations for our livestock. It will also create job opportunities for local people, support ecological restoration, and help improve our food systems.

Your donation will have a significant impact by allowing us to drive this project forward at a faster pace. It will help fund essential initiatives, such as sustainable farming practices, Bible Education Centers, and community support programs, enabling us to make a lasting difference in Cambodia and beyond.

The long-term goals of our project are to create a self-sustaining farm that provides support to the local community through sustainable farming practices. We aim to fund and build Bible Education Centers, create job opportunities for locals, and develop a space for Bible school events. Once we start earning money, we also hope to extend our support to other countries. Ultimately, we strive to strengthen the work of preaching in Cambodia and promote long-lasting, positive change for the community.

Yes, there are several ways you can contribute to our mission! You can donate monthly, offer in-kind donations, or volunteer your time. Volunteers can stay at the farm and teach the locals the Bible, or help with farm improvements. Every form of support makes a big difference, and we are grateful for your involvement!

We provide regular updates through our website, social media, and newsletters. As a donor, you will receive quarterly reports detailing how your contribution has been used and the impact it has made. We will also share success stories and project milestones with our community.

Yes, all donations made to our project are tax-deductible, as we are a registered nonprofit organization. You will receive a receipt for your contribution, which can be used for your tax filings.

Our project is still in its early stages, but we are committed to building a strong foundation for sustainable farming and community development in Cambodia. We have successfully begun implementing key initiatives such as acquiring land for the farm, setting up eco-friendly practices, and developing partnerships to support our mission. As we grow, we will continue to share updates on our progress and the impact of your support.
